Tokenomics

$Miku tokenomics are designed to empower the community and drive long-term value. With a limited supply, every $Miku held becomes more valuable. Our deflationary mechanisms promote scarcity and reward holders with automatic burns and redistribution.
A portion of each transaction is burned, reducing the total supply over time. This scarcity increases the value of existing $Miku tokens. Additionally, a percentage is redistributed to existing holders, incentivizing continued support and loyalty.
